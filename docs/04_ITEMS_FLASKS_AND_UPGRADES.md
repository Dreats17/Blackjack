## Durability System

Items degrade with use. At zero durability, items **break** and lose all effects.

| Repair Method | Where | Notes |
|--------|-------|-------|
| Car Workbench | Your car (Tool Kit) | Full repairs |
| Super Glue | Field | Quick fix |
| Duct Tape | Field | Quick fix |
| Oswald's Shop | Oswald's | Premium upgrades |
