## Item System Overview

### Inventory
- Items are stored in a **set** — you either have an item or you don't (no stacking).
- Items can be: **active** (in inventory), **broken** (used up durability), or **repairing** (at a mechanic).
- Broken items can be taken to a mechanic for repair.

### Item States
| State | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| Active | In your inventory, providing effects |
| Broken | Durability depleted, no longer functional |
| Repairing | At a mechanic shop, awaiting repair |

## Flask Potions

Flasks are **temporary magical effects** purchased from the **Witch Doctor's Tower**. Each flask has limited durability (typically 4 uses) and provides a unique gameplay effect.

### Available Flasks

| Flask | Effect | Uses |
|-------|--------|------|
| **No Bust** | Prevents going over 21 during Blackjack | 4 |
| **Imminent Blackjack** | Increases chances of natural 21 | 4 |
| **Dealer's Whispers** | Hints at the Dealer's hidden card | 4 |
| **Bonus Fortune** | Grants Double Down ability (like Gambler's Chalice) | 4 |
| **Anti-Venom** | Cures poison status effects | 4 |
| **Anti-Virus** | Cures illness status effects | 4 |
| **Fortunate Day** | Better random event outcomes during the day | 4 |
| **Fortunate Night** | Better random event outcomes at night | 4 |
| **Second Chance** | Replay one losing hand per session (asked yes/no) | 4 |
| **Split Serum** | Grants Split ability (like Twin's Locket) | 4 |
| **Dealer's Hesitation** | 66% chance to force Dealer to draw extra when at 17–20 | 4 |
| **Pocket Aces** | Guarantees your first card is an Ace (single use, then removed) | 1 |

### Flask Mixing Warning

Carrying **3+ active flask effects** at once triggers a mixing check:
- **50% chance:** You puke up all flask effects (all removed)
- **10–40 HP damage** from the magical reaction
- **Possible death** if HP is too low

> **Tip:** Keep to 1–2 flasks at a time to avoid mixing sickness.

### Where to Buy

Flasks are sold at the **Witch Doctor's Tower** (available as an afternoon location). Each flask has a **66% chance** of being in stock on any given visit.

## Durability System

Most upgradeable items and flasks have **durability** — they break after a certain number of uses.

### How Durability Works

1. Each item has a durability counter (stored in `_item_durability` array).
2. When an item effect triggers, durability is consumed.
3. When durability hits 0, the item **breaks** — it's moved from active inventory to broken inventory.
4. Broken items can be **repaired** at a mechanic shop for a cost.

### Durability Values

**Base items** have lower durability than **upgraded items**. Specific values vary per item, but generally:
- Base items: ~15–25 uses
- Upgraded items: ~45–70 uses

### Flask Durability

Flasks typically have **4 uses** each before they're depleted and removed from active effects.

### Repair Process

1. Visit a mechanic (Tom, Frank, or Oswald).
2. Pay the repair cost ($3,000–$25,000 depending on mechanic and item).
3. **50% success rate** on repair attempt.
4. On failure, the mechanic keeps your money but the item remains broken.
5. Oswald has the best success rate and also offers upgrades.
