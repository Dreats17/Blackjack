## Adventure Locations (Rank 2+)

### The Road
Cee-lo dice, hitchhikers, roadside shrines, broken-down bus, road dogs, casual walks.

### The Woodlands
Hunting competition, gigantic bear, fountain of youth, hermit cabin, animal whistle companions.

### The Swamp
Tortoise racing, ogre encounter, fairy bottle, mermaid, gator wrestling.

### The Beach
Swimming, diving, treasure hunting.

### The City
Urban encounters, street events.

### The Ocean Depths
Deep underwater adventures (high rank).
