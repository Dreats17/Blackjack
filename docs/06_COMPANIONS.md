# 06: Companion System

## Overview

8 animal companions with unique passive bonuses to sanity, luck, income, and security. Each has a happiness system with **4 mood levels** (Bonded, Happy, Neutral, Sad) and **24 unique dialogue lines** (6 per mood). Neglect risks permanent loss; nurturing unlocks powerful bonuses and hidden endings.

---

## All 8 Companions

### 1. Squirrelly (Squirrel)

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Favorite Food** | Bag of Acorns ($10) |
| **Bonuses** | +2 sanity/day, +1 luck |
| **Found** | Random squirrel event |

Loves acorns obsessively, builds nests in your car. Reliable early-game companion.

### 2. Whiskers (Alley Cat)

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Favorite Food** | Cat Food |
| **Bonuses** | +3 sanity/day, danger warning |
| **Found** | Stray cat event or vet storyline |

**Unique Ability:** Warns about upcoming dangerous events before they happen.

### 3. Lucky (Three-Legged Dog)

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Favorite Food** | Dog Food |
| **Bonuses** | +5 sanity/day, physical protection |
| **Found** | Random encounter or Lost Dog questline |

**Unique Ability:** Intervenes during attacks, shielding the player. Highest sanity bonus.

**Personal Storyline (4 stages):**
1. Scars Discovery
2. Previous Owner revealed
3. Learns to Trust
4. Saves Your Life

### 4. Mr. Pecks (Crow)

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Favorite Food** | Birdseed ($4) |
| **Bonuses** | +1 sanity/day, 5% money finding |
| **Found** | Random crow encounter |

**Unique Ability:** 5% chance per event to find extra cash. Recruits a murder of crows over time.

### 5. Patches (Opossum)

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Favorite Food** | Garbage (free) |
| **Bonuses** | +2 sanity/day, night bonuses |
| **Found** | Random opossum encounter |

**Unique Ability:** Enhanced nighttime event outcomes. Most budget-friendly companion.

### 6. Rusty (Raccoon)

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Favorite Food** | Anything (flexible) |
| **Bonuses** | +2 sanity/day, 3% steal chance |
| **Found** | Random raccoon encounter |

**Unique Ability:** 3% chance to steal items during encounters.

### 7. Slick (Rat)

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Favorite Food** | Cheese ($6) |
| **Bonuses** | +1 sanity/day, danger warning + escape routes |
| **Found** | Random rat encounter |

**Unique Ability:** Identifies escape routes and detects approaching threats.

### 8. Hopper (Rabbit)

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Favorite Food** | Carrot |
| **Bonuses** | +2 sanity/day, +3 luck |
| **Found** | Random rabbit encounter |

**Unique Ability:** Highest luck bonus of any companion (+3).

## Happiness & Mood System

| Mood | Bonus Level | Description |
|------|-------------|-------------|
| **Bonded** | Maximum + special dialogue | Deepest trust |
| **Happy** | Full bonuses | Content and effective |
| **Neutral** | Reduced bonuses | Indifferent |
| **Sad** | Minimal; runaway risk | May leave permanently |

---

## Secret Sanctuary Ending

**Requirements:** 5+ companions + Animal Whistle + $1,000,000

A hidden airfield appears. Choose to establish an **animal sanctuary** — one of the game's most wholesome endings.

---

## Dark Path: Companion Selling

| Step | Achievement |
|------|-------------|
| Sell 1 | "First Blood" |
| Sell 2 | "The Product" |
| Sell 3+ | "Meat Cubes" |

Selling 3+ unlocks **"The Factory"** dark ending. Sold companions never return. Mutually exclusive with Sanctuary ending.

---

## Strategy Tips

- **Budget choice:** Patches (free food)
- **Best sanity:** Lucky (+5/day)
- **Best luck:** Hopper (+3)
- **Best income:** Mr. Pecks (5% money find)
- **Sanctuary path:** Keep 5+ companions happy + find Animal Whistle
