## HOW COMPANIONS WORK

Companions are animals you encounter through random events, storylines, and exploration. Once befriended, they travel with you in your wagon, providing passive bonuses, emotional support, and unique dialogue. There is **no hard limit** on how many companions you can have.

Each companion has:
- **Happiness** (0–100): Determines mood, bonuses, and whether they stay
- **Days Owned**: How long you've been together
- **Fed Today**: Whether you've fed them this day cycle
- **Bonded**: Special permanent status earned through sustained high happiness

## COMPANION ROSTER

### 🐿️ Squirrelly — Squirrel
- **How to Find**: Random event (various tiers)
- **Favorite Food**: Bag of Acorns
- **Bonuses**: Sanity Restore +2, Luck Bonus +1
- **Personality**: Energetic, gift-giving, acorn-obsessed
- **Special**: Can become the casino mascot in the Transcendence ending. Rides on your shoulder.

### 🐱 Whiskers — Alley Cat
- **How to Find**: "Stray Cat" storyline (starts in Poor tier events)
- **Favorite Food**: Cat Food
- **Bonuses**: Sanity Restore +3, Danger Warning
- **Personality**: Aloof but deeply affectionate once bonded. Senses danger before it arrives.
- **Special**: Has a multi-part storyline (stray_cat arc) — can get sick, may have kittens, or may die depending on your choices.

### 🐕 Lucky — Three-Legged Dog
- **How to Find**: "Three-Legged Dog" event (Poor tier) or the Lucky Dog storyline
- **Favorite Food**: Dog Food
- **Bonuses**: Sanity Restore +5, Protection
- **Personality**: Unbreakable spirit. Lost a leg but never lost his joy. Will die for you.
- **Special**: Has a dedicated 5-part storyline (lucky_dog arc) revealing his past and the previous owner who hurt him. Highest sanity restore of common companions.

### 🐦‍⬛ Mr. Pecks — Crow
- **How to Find**: Random events
- **Favorite Food**: Birdseed
- **Bonuses**: Find Money Chance (5%), Sanity Restore +1
- **Personality**: Wickedly intelligent. Remembers every face. Brings you shiny trinkets and, eventually, actual money.
- **Special**: When bonded, recruits an entire murder of crows to watch over you.

### 🦝 Rusty — Raccoon
- **How to Find**: Random events
- **Favorite Food**: Anything
- **Bonuses**: Steal Chance (3%), Sanity Restore +2
- **Personality**: Mischievous, dexterous, a born thief. Opens your glove box, your wallet, and eventually other people's wallets.
- **Special**: When bonded, builds a raccoon crime network. Steals exclusively for you.

### 🐀 Slick — Rat
- **How to Find**: "Sewer Rat" event (Poor tier)
- **Favorite Food**: Cheese
- **Bonuses**: Danger Warning, Sanity Restore +1
- **Personality**: Surprisingly clean and clever. Memorizes every escape route in every location. 
- **Special**: When bonded, creates a network of rat informants. Alerts you with specific squeaks for different threats.

### 🦔 Patches — Opossum
- **How to Find**: "Opossum in Trash" event (Poor tier)
- **Favorite Food**: Garbage
- **Bonuses**: Night Bonus (extra sanity + less fatigue at night), Sanity Restore +2
- **Personality**: Nocturnal. Plays dead when scared. The fact that they *stop* playing dead around you is the highest trust.
- **Special**: Night guardian — keeps watch while you sleep, reducing fatigue and restoring sanity.

### 🐰 Hopper — Rabbit
- **How to Find**: Random events
- **Favorite Food**: Carrot
- **Bonuses**: Luck Bonus +3, Sanity Restore +2
- **Personality**: Does binkies (joyful hops). Claims your entire car as their territory.
- **Special**: Highest luck bonus of all companions. Things just seem to go right when Hopper is around.

### 🦅 Squawk — Seagull
- **How to Find**: Random events
- **Favorite Food**: Anything (seagulls eat everything)
- **Bonuses**: Sanity Restore +1
- **Personality**: Loud, possessive ("MINE! MINE! MINE!"), lands on your head constantly. Annoying. You love it.

### 🦆 General Quackers — Duck
- **How to Find**: Random events
- **Favorite Food**: Bread
- **Bonuses**: Sanity Restore +2
- **Personality**: Military-grade discipline. Gives respectful salutes. You're the commanding officer.

### 🕊️ Don Coo — Pigeon
- **How to Find**: Random events
- **Favorite Food**: Birdseed
- **Bonuses**: Sanity Restore +1
- **Personality**: Struts with authority. A pigeon of distinction and leadership.

### 🦌 Grace — Deer (with Fawns)
- **How to Find**: Random forest/nature events
- **Favorite Food**: Apples
- **Bonuses**: Sanity Restore +5
- **Personality**: Gentle, peaceful. Travels with her fawns. Being near Grace is the most peaceful you'll feel.
- **Special**: Extra sanity restore when interacting (+5 bonus). Fawns bring you wildflowers at the Sanctuary.

### 🐻 Bruno / Ursus — Bear
- **How to Find**: Adventure events, rare encounters
- **Favorite Food**: Fish / Honey
- **Bonuses**: Sanity Restore +3, Protection
- **Personality**: Massive, warm, protective. Lets you lean against them. You're under their protection now.
- **Special**: Guards the valley at the Sanctuary ending. Nothing dares approach.

### 🐍 Noodle — Snake

- **Favorite Food**: Mice
- **Bonuses**: Sanity Restore +1
- **Personality**: Coils around your arm. Gives "snake kisses" (tongue flicks). Grumpy when hungry.

### 🐊 Chomper — Alligator
- **How to Find**: Swamp/water adventure events
- **Favorite Food**: Meat
- **Bonuses**: Protection, Sanity Restore +2
- **Personality**: Floats with just eyes above water, watching you protectively. It should be creepy. It's kind of sweet.

### 🦀 Deathclaw — Crab
- **How to Find**: Beach/water events
- **Favorite Food**: Seaweed
- **Bonuses**: Sanity Restore +1
- **Personality**: Does sideways victory dances. Waves pincers aggressively when hungry.

### 🐢 Speedy — Tortoise
- **How to Find**: Random events
- **Favorite Food**: Lettuce
- **Bonuses**: Sanity Restore +2
- **Personality**: Ancient, patient. Makes tiny satisfied squeaks. Time moves slower with tortoises.

### 🐢 Shellbert — Ancient Tortoise
- **How to Find**: Rare events
- **Favorite Food**: Lettuce
- **Bonuses**: Sanity Restore +6
- **Personality**: Gazes with wise, patient eyes. Looking into them, you understand: everything will be okay. Eventually.

### 🐇 Moonwhisker — Magical Rabbit
- **How to Find**: Rare/mystical events
- **Favorite Food**: Moonberries
- **Bonuses**: Sanity Restore +7
- **Personality**: Leaves glowing pawprints that fade after a few seconds. Distinctly magical.
- **Special**: Highest sanity restore per interaction of any companion.

### 🦦 Scooter — Otter
- **How to Find**: Water/river events
- **Favorite Food**: Fish
- **Bonuses**: Sanity Restore +3
- **Personality**: Floats on their back doing flips. Chirps happily. Otters are the happiest creatures on earth.

### 🐬 Echo — Dolphin

- **Favorite Food**: Fish
- **Bonuses**: Sanity Restore +10, Healing +10
- **Personality**: Possibly the smartest creature on Earth. Knows exactly when you need comfort.
- **Special**: Highest combined sanity + health bonus of any companion during interaction.

### 🦑 Kraken — Legendary Sea Creature
- **How to Find**: Extremely rare deep water events
- **Favorite Food**: ???
- **Bonuses**: Sanity Restore +8
- **Personality**: Ancient. Terrible. Somehow befriended. Pats your head with a tentacle.
- **Special**: Emerges from the river at the Sanctuary. Visits from the deep to witness your passing in the Salvation ending.

### 🐴 Thunder — Horse
- **How to Find**: Jameson's Ranch storyline or rare events

- **Bonuses**: Sanity Restore +4, Protection
- **Personality**: Proud, noble. Nickers softly. The bond transcends words.

### 🐄 Betsy — Cow
- **How to Find**: Betsy storyline (hungry_cow → starving_cow → cow_army)
- **Favorite Food**: Grass / Hay
- **Bonuses**: Sanity Restore +2
- **Personality**: Moos contentedly. Stares at your wallet (old habits).

### 🐟 Bubbles — Goldfish

- **Favorite Food**: Fish Flakes
- **Bonuses**: Sanity Restore +4
- **Personality**: Swims in lazy circles, scales shimmering like liquid gold. Watching them is meditative.

### 🦝 Don — Raccoon Boss

## HAPPINESS SYSTEM

| Happiness Range | Mood | Effects |
|---|---|---|
| **80–100** | Happy/Bonded | Full bonuses active, positive dialogue, bonding possible |
| **50–79** | Content | Most bonuses active, neutral dialogue |
| **40–49** | Neutral | Reduced bonuses, mixed dialogue |
| **11–39** | Sad | Minimal bonuses, sad dialogue, risk of running away |
| **0–10** | Miserable | No bonuses, 33% daily chance of **running away** |

### How Happiness Changes
| Action | Effect |
|---|---|
| Feed companion | +10 happiness (with food item), +5 (without) |
| Pet/Play | +3 happiness |
| Sit together quietly | +5 sanity restored to you |
| Don't feed (daily) | **-5 happiness** |
| Companion fed today (already) | No additional feeding bonus |

## BONDING

A companion becomes **Bonded** (♥) when:
1. Their happiness has been **80 or higher**
2. You've owned them for **7 or more days**

**Bonded companions**:
- Display a ♥ symbol next to their name
- Have unique "bonded" dialogue (the most heartfelt lines)
- Their bond is described as "unbreakable"
- May have enhanced bonus effects
- Featured prominently in ending sequences

## FEEDING & CARE

### Daily Cycle
- Each day, companions' `fed_today` flag resets
- If they were **not fed** the previous day, they lose **5 happiness**
- Feed them once per day to prevent happiness decay

### Feeding Methods
1. **During afternoon companion interaction**: Choose "Feed them"
2. **Group feeding**: "Spend time with all of them" feeds everyone
3. **Specific food items**: Some companions have favorite foods (listed above)

### Afternoon Interactions
During the afternoon phase, you can:
1. Pick a specific companion to interact with
2. Spend time with all of them (group activity — feeds and pets everyone, restores 10 sanity and 15 health)
3. Skip and head out

Individual interactions offer:
1. **Feed them** — +10 happiness, +5 health to you
2. **Pet/Play** — +3 happiness, +3 sanity to you
3. **Just sit together** — +5 sanity, +10 health to you
4. **Leave them be** — No effect

## COMPANION BONUSES

Bonuses are applied passively during the day and night cycles.

### Day Bonuses (apply_companion_day_bonuses)
| Bonus Type | How It Works | Example Companions |
|---|---|---|
| **Sanity Restore** | 33% chance/day to restore sanity (if companion happiness ≥50) | All companions |
| **Find Money** | % chance to find $1–$20 (if happiness ≥40) | Mr. Pecks (5%) |
| **Steal Chance** | % chance to find $2–$15 (if happiness ≥50) | Rusty (3%) |
| **Luck Bonus** | % chance to find $5–$25 (if happiness ≥50) | Hopper (3%), Squirrelly (1%) |

### Night Bonuses (apply_companion_night_bonuses)
| Bonus Type | How It Works | Example Companions |
|---|---|---|
| **Night Bonus** | 33% chance: +3 sanity, -1 fatigue (if happiness ≥50) | Patches |

### Companion Dialogue
- **20% chance per day** to hear a random companion's dialogue
- Dialogue varies by mood: happy, neutral, sad, or bonded
- Each companion has **6 lines per mood** (happy/neutral/sad) and **4 bonded lines**
- Dialogue is displayed in cyan text

## LOSING COMPANIONS

### Running Away
- If happiness drops to **10 or below**, there's a **33% chance per day** the companion runs away
- Status changes to "lost"
- You lose **10 sanity** when a companion runs away

### Death
- Companions can die through specific story events or dangerous encounters
- Status changes to "dead"
- You lose **20 sanity** when a companion dies
- Dead companions are acknowledged in ending sequences

### Prevention
- Feed companions daily (prevents the -5 happiness decay)
- Interact with them during afternoons
- Keep happiness above 10 at all times to prevent runaway risk

## SELLING COMPANIONS (DARK PATH)

At **Grimy Gus's Pawn Shop**, you can sell living companions for cash. This is considered the "dark path" and has consequences:

- Gus will buy any companion you're willing to part with
- Prices vary by companion rarity and type
- Selling companions **decreases your sanity** significantly
- Some companions may trigger special guilt dialogue
- The game remembers what you've done

> *"You sold a living creature for gambling money. The Dealer noticed. Everyone noticed."*

## THE SANCTUARY ENDING

The **Sanctuary** is a secret ending available at the airport when you have:
1. **5 or more living companions**
2. The **Animal Whistle** item

Instead of boarding a regular plane, you can choose to **charter a private plane** for you and all your animals. This costs $100,000 (or whatever you have if you can't afford it).

You fly to a hidden valley — **The Sanctuary** — where other "shepherds" live with their own animal families. It's a place of peace, protection, and found family.

The ending lists all your companions and their bond status, and features companion-specific moments based on who you've collected.

> **Ending Title**: *SANCTUARY — SECRET ENDING: THE SHEPHERD*
