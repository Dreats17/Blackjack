ENDING #1: SALVATION (Best Ending / "Going Home")
------------------------------------------------------------------------------
REQUIREMENTS:
  ☑ Progress through game until you meet Tom the Mechanic
  ☑ Tom mentions he found a number in your car
  ☑ Special event triggers where you call Rebecca
  ☑ Rebecca answers (she's your wife)
  ☑ Choose: "Yes, I want to come home"
  ☑ Do NOT choose to keep gambling

STEP-BY-STEP UNLOCK:
  1. Survive to Rank 1+ (Modest tier: $1,000+)
  2. Trigger "Tom the Mechanic" one-time event (day event in Rank 1)
  3. Visit Tom's Trucks & Tires shop
  4. Complete his dialogue and dream sequences
  5. Event "Rebecca's Number" triggers (Tom found it in your car)
  6. Make the phone call choice
  7. Rebecca answers, emotional scene
  8. CRITICAL CHOICE: "Do you want to come home?"
     → Choose: [YES] (this is the salvation path)
     → Refuse: [NO] (leads to Resurrection ending instead)
  9. Ending sequence plays

ENDING NARRATIVE:
  - You leave the casino forever
  - Drive home to your family
  - Rebecca is waiting
  - Your son Nathan runs to greet you
  - He says "Dada!" (his first word)
  - Watch him grow up: first steps, school, graduation
  - You attend his wedding
  - Die peacefully surrounded by family
  - Final line: "You made it home."

ACHIEVEMENTS UNLOCKED:
  • Going Home
  • Family Man
  • Best Ending
  • Saved

TIPS:
  - This ending is available even if you're broke
  - No money requirement
  - Just need to make the right choice when given the chance
  - Most emotionally satisfying ending
  - True "win" condition

BLACKJACK MECHANICS:
================================================================================

BASIC RULES:
• Goal: Get closer to 21 than dealer without going over
• Face cards = 10, Aces = 1 or 11
• Blackjack (Ace + 10) pays 3:2
• Win = 1x bet, Lose = lose bet, Push = tie (no loss)

ACTIONS:
• Hit: Take another card
• Stand: Keep current hand
• Double Down: Double bet, take one card, end turn
• Split: If two same cards, split into two hands (double bet)
• Surrender: Give up half bet, save half (available sometimes)
• Insurance: If dealer shows Ace, bet on dealer blackjack

ADVANCED STRATEGIES:
• Basic Strategy Chart: Optimal play for every situation
• Card Counting: Track high/low cards (difficult, risky if caught)
• Bet Sizing: Increase bets when winning, decrease when losing
• Bankroll Management: Never bet more than 5% of balance
• Table Selection: Find tables with good rules

DEALER BEHAVIORS:
• Normal Dealer: Standard rules, fair play
• The Dealer (Jade Eye): Special NPC, mysterious, rule-bending
• Drunk Dealer: Makes mistakes (can help or hurt you)
• Strict Dealer: No favors, by-the-book
• Friendly Dealer: May give hints, more forgiving

CASINO HEAT:
• Win too much = Casino attention
• Card counting detected = Banned (or worse)
• Accusations = Forced to leave, possible violence
• VIP Status = More freedom, higher limits
• Balance reputation with winnings!
