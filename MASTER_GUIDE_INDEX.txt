MONEY RANKS (6 tiers):
• Rank 0: Poor ($1-$1,000)

• Rank 4: Rich ($250,000-$1,000,000)
• Rank 5: Millionaire ($1,000,000+)

Each rank unlocks new events, shops, and story content.

HEALTH SYSTEM (0-100 HP):
• 100 HP = Perfect health
• 75-99 HP = Good health
• 50-74 HP = Fair health
• 25-49 HP = Poor health
• 10-24 HP = Critical health
• 0-9 HP = Near death
• 0 HP = Death (usually)

Lose HP from: Injuries, illnesses, accidents, neglect
Gain HP from: Food, medicine, rest, doctor visits

SANITY SYSTEM (0-100 Sanity):
• 100 = Perfectly sane
• 80-99 = Stable
• 60-79 = Stressed
• 40-59 = Unstable
• 20-39 = Breaking
• 10-19 = Nearly broken
• 0-9 = Madness threshold
• 0 = Madness Confrontation event

Lose Sanity from: Trauma, loss, horror events, isolation
Gain Sanity from: Positive events, companions, beauty, kindness

DEALER HAPPINESS (0-100):
• 0-20 = Hostile (DANGEROUS)
• 21-40 = Angry
• 41-60 = Neutral
• 61-80 = Pleasant
• 81-100 = Friendly

Affects: Dealer behavior, dialogue, special events, endings
Increase: Gift items, win politely, good behavior
Decrease: Lose repeatedly, fraudulent cash, insults

COMPANION SYSTEM:
• 32 possible companions
• Each needs daily care (food)
• Happiness: 0-100
• Benefits: Sanity boost, stat bonuses, companionship
• Can die if neglected
• Can be sold (BETRAYAL PATH)

ACHIEVEMENT SYSTEM:
• 240+ achievements total
• Track progress and milestones
• Hidden achievements unlock secrets
• Some achievements are mutually exclusive
• Completing all = "Completionist" achievement

ENDING EVENTS:

• Examples: Millionaire morning visitor, Rebecca's call

LOCATION EVENTS:

• Examples: Beach adventures, swamp exploration
• Can repeat, but variations occur

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
================================================================================

Q: How long does it take to beat the game?
A: 20-100+ hours depending on playstyle and which ending you pursue.

Q: Can I get all achievements in one playthrough?
A: No. Some achievements are mutually exclusive. Plan for 3-5 playthroughs.

Q: What's the "best" ending?
A: Subjective. Salvation is most uplifting. Millionaire feels victorious.
   Resurrection is hauntingly beautiful. Each has merit.

Q: Is there a "true" ending?
A: No official true ending. All 8 are valid conclusions.

Q: Can I save my game?
A: [Depends on implementation - check game features]

Q: What happens if I die?
A: Death ending triggers. Game over. Some death scenes are unique.

Q: Can companions come back after death?
A: Rarely. Companion_reunion event can happen but is rare.

Q: How do I increase Dealer happiness?
A: Gift items (expensive ones work best), win politely, visit consistently.

Q: Should I use the loan shark?
A: Dangerous but useful. Can spiral into debt. Use cautiously.

Q: What's the fastest way to $1M?
A: High-stakes gambling with potions, but very risky. Conservative play
   is slower but safer.

Q: Can I max all stats?
A: With upgrades, yes. Oswald's shop allows item enhancement to +10.

Q: Are there romance options?
A: No traditional romance. Relationships are platonic/professional.

Q: Can I kill NPCs?
A: No. But some NPCs can die in events. Phil can escalate to death.

Q: What's the darkest content?
A: The Factory ending. Selling companions. Some medical events are graphic.

Q: Is there a difficulty setting?
A: No explicit setting, but your choices affect difficulty.

Q: Can I replay events?
A: Random events can repeat. Story events are one-time only.
