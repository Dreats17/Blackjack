1. GAME OVERVIEW
================================================================================

PREMISE:
----------------------------------------
You are a gambler living in your station wagon, trying to win $1,000,000
at the local casino's blackjack table. Each day consists of:

  MORNING/AFTERNOON: Random events occur based on your current wealth tier
  EVENING: You travel to the casino (if you have a working car)
  NIGHT: You play blackjack against The Dealer
  LATE NIGHT: Night events can occur as you return to your car

CORE MECHANICS:
----------------------------------------
  - HEALTH (0-100): Your physical wellbeing. Reaches 0 = Death
  - SANITY (0-100): Your mental state. Low sanity causes hallucinations
  - BALANCE ($): Your current money. Goal is $1,000,000
  - INVENTORY: Items that provide benefits or are required for events
  - STATUS EFFECTS: Temporary conditions affecting gameplay
  - INJURIES: Physical wounds that may require medical attention
  - COMPANIONS: Pets that provide bonuses and restore sanity

WIN CONDITION: Reach $1,000,000
LOSE CONDITIONS: Health reaches 0, certain bad endings, giving up

The game has multiple endings based on your choices and conditions.

----------------------------------------------------------------------
GOOD ENDINGS:
----------------------------------------------------------------------

  SALVATION (Best Ending)
    Requirement: Call Rebecca at Tom's, choose to go home
    Outcome: Return to family, live a full life, watch kids grow

  MILLIONAIRE (Goal Ending)
    Requirement: Reach $1,000,000
    Outcome: Achieve your goal, various epilogues possible

----------------------------------------------------------------------
NEUTRAL ENDINGS:
----------------------------------------------------------------------

  RESURRECTION
    Requirement: Refuse to go home when given the chance
    Outcome: Continue gambling forever, outcome unknown

  THE OFFER ACCEPTED
    Requirement: Accept the mysterious offer at $900k+
    Outcome: Get double your money, but banned from casinos

----------------------------------------------------------------------
BAD ENDINGS:
----------------------------------------------------------------------

  DEATH (Multiple Causes)
    - Health reaches 0
    - Killed by NPCs (Cow Army, Phil, Loan Shark)
    - Medical emergencies untreated
    - Car accidents
    - Random deadly events

  MADNESS ENDING
    Requirement: Fail the Madness Confrontation at low sanity
    Outcome: Your mind shatters, something else takes control
    Note: This is a secret ending with extensive narrative

  SUZY THE SNITCH
    Requirement: Complete Suzy storyline on bad path
    Outcome: Suzy reports you to police, arrested

15. SANITY SYSTEM
================================================================================

Sanity ranges from 0-100 and affects your perception of events.

----------------------------------------------------------------------
SANITY THRESHOLDS:
----------------------------------------------------------------------
  100-76: Normal - No effects
  75-51:  Uneasy - Occasional unsettling thoughts
  50-26:  Disturbed - Hallucinations, altered perception
  25-1:   Broken - Severe effects, madness confrontation risk
  0:      Catatonic - Cannot function

----------------------------------------------------------------------
SANITY EFFECTS:
----------------------------------------------------------------------
  - Low sanity changes event descriptions
  - Hallucinations appear as fake events
  - Companions may appear to speak
  - The Dealer's dialogue becomes more sinister
  - At very low sanity, the Madness Confrontation occurs

----------------------------------------------------------------------
RESTORING SANITY:
----------------------------------------------------------------------
  - Companions restore sanity daily
  - Positive events can restore sanity
  - Good sleep restores sanity
  - Certain items restore sanity
  - The Doctor can prescribe treatment
