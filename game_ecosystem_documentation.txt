1. GAME OVERVIEW
================================================================================

PREMISE:
----------------------------------------
You are a gambler living in your station wagon, trying to win $1,000,000
at the local casino's blackjack table. Each day consists of:

  MORNING/AFTERNOON: Random events occur based on your current wealth tier
  EVENING: You travel to the casino (if you have a working car)
  NIGHT: You play blackjack against The Dealer
  LATE NIGHT: Night events can occur as you return to your car

CORE MECHANICS:
----------------------------------------
  - HEALTH (0-100): Your physical wellbeing. Reaches 0 = Death
  - SANITY (0-100): Your mental state. Low sanity causes hallucinations
  - BALANCE ($): Your current money. Goal is $1,000,000
  - INVENTORY: Items that provide benefits or are required for events
  - STATUS EFFECTS: Temporary conditions affecting gameplay
  - INJURIES: Physical wounds that may require medical attention
  - COMPANIONS: Pets that provide bonuses and restore sanity

WIN CONDITION: Reach $1,000,000
LOSE CONDITIONS: Health reaches 0, certain bad endings, giving up

3. CORE SYSTEMS
================================================================================

----------------------------------------------------------------------
HEALTH SYSTEM (0-100):
----------------------------------------------------------------------
  - Your physical wellbeing
  - Damage from: injuries, illnesses, events, starvation
  - Healing: First Aid Kit (+30), Bandages (+15), Doctor visits, rest
  - Death at 0 HP triggers game over + achievement summary
  - Can track health via status command

----------------------------------------------------------------------
SANITY SYSTEM (0-100):
----------------------------------------------------------------------
  - Your mental state and perception of reality
  - Loss from: loneliness, traumatic events, cursed items, isolation
  - Restoration: companions (+1-5 daily), positive events, rest
  - Effects by tier:
    → 100-76: Normal - no effects
    → 75-51: Uneasy - occasional disturbing thoughts
    → 50-26: Disturbed - hallucinations, altered event text
    → 25-1:  Broken - severe mental effects, gameplay changes
    → 0:     Madness Confrontation triggered (special ending path)
  - Low sanity changes Dealer dialogue and event descriptions
  - "Broken" status at very low sanity affects all gameplay

----------------------------------------------------------------------
BALANCE SYSTEM ($):
----------------------------------------------------------------------
  - Your current money (goal: $1,000,000)
  - Two types: REAL cash and FRAUDULENT cash (tracked separately)
  - Earned from: gambling wins, events, selling items/collectibles
  - Lost through: gambling losses, purchases, loan repayments, events
  - Displayed as: "$5,000 (+ $2,000 fraudulent)" when applicable
  - Balance determines your rank and available events

----------------------------------------------------------------------
INVENTORY SYSTEM:
----------------------------------------------------------------------
  - Holds items, collectibles, consumables, tools
  - Items can be: used, broken, upgraded, sold, gifted
  - Some items have durability and break after uses
  - Special items unlock events or protect from dangers
  - Collectibles can be sold to Gus or "garbled" for mystery rewards
  - Max capacity unclear (can hoard 50+ items based on achievements)

----------------------------------------------------------------------
COMPANION SYSTEM:
----------------------------------------------------------------------
  - Befriend animals to restore sanity and gain bonuses
  - 32 different companions available (see Section 14)
  - Each needs specific food to stay happy
  - Daily happiness changes (-1 per day if not fed)
  - Companions at 0 happiness will leave
  - Afternoon dialogue system (250+ lines with happiness variants)
  - Can pet, feed, and interact with companions
  - Betrayal path: Can sell companions to Gus one-by-one (see Section 21)

----------------------------------------------------------------------
STATUS EFFECTS:
----------------------------------------------------------------------
  - Temporary conditions affecting gameplay
  - Positive: Lucky, Energized, Blessed, At Peace
  - Negative: Sick, Injured, Cursed, Addicted, Marked
  - Can stack multiple effects
  - Some effects prevent travel or casino access
  - Doctor can cure most negative statuses

----------------------------------------------------------------------
INJURIES SYSTEM:
----------------------------------------------------------------------
  - Physical wounds requiring medical attention
  - Examples: Broken Ribs, Concussion, Deep Laceration
  - Cause continuous health drain
  - Can become infected if untreated
  - Doctor visits required to heal serious injuries
  - Some injuries prevent activities

10. ENDING PATHS (ALL 8 MAJOR ENDINGS)
================================================================================

The game has 8 distinct ending paths based on choices and conditions.
Each ending has unique narrative and achievement possibilities.

----------------------------------------------------------------------
1. SALVATION (Best Ending)
----------------------------------------------------------------------
  Trigger: Call Rebecca at Tom's shop, choose to go home
  Requirements: 
    - Found Rebecca's number at Tom's
    - Made the choice to leave gambling behind
  Outcome: 
    - Return to family
    - Watch Nathan grow up (first steps, first word: "Dada")
    - Full life with wife and children
    - Peaceful death surrounded by family
  Achievement: "Going Home" / "Family Man"
  Narrative: Most emotionally satisfying, redemption arc

----------------------------------------------------------------------
2. MILLIONAIRE (Goal Ending)
----------------------------------------------------------------------
  Trigger: Reach $1,000,000
  Requirements:
    - Win enough at blackjack to hit $1,000,000
  Outcome:
    - Achieve your goal
    - The Dealer acknowledges your victory
    - Multiple epilogue variants based on companion count
    - If companions present: They celebrate with you
  Achievement: "Millionaire" / "The Dream"
  Narrative: You did it, you won the game

----------------------------------------------------------------------
3. RESURRECTION (Mysterious Ending)
----------------------------------------------------------------------
  Trigger: Refuse Rebecca's call, choose to stay gambling
  Requirements:
    - Found Rebecca's number BUT refused to go home
    - Chosen gambling over family
  Outcome:
    - Continue gambling indefinitely
    - Trapped in eternal cycle
    - Ambiguous fate
  Achievement: "The Cycle Continues"
  Narrative: Did you make the right choice?

----------------------------------------------------------------------
4. MADNESS (Horror Ending)
----------------------------------------------------------------------
  Trigger: Fail Madness Confrontation at very low sanity
  Requirements:
    - Sanity below 25 for 5+ consecutive days
    - Face yourself in the mirror event
    - Lose the confrontation
  Outcome:
    - Your mind shatters completely
    - "Something else" takes control
    - You become a passenger in your own body
    - Extensive horror narrative
  Achievement: "Lost to Madness" / "Madness Consumed"
  Narrative: Psychological horror, identity death

----------------------------------------------------------------------
5. THE OFFER ACCEPTED (Deal Ending)
----------------------------------------------------------------------
  Trigger: Accept mysterious offer at $250,000+
  Requirements:
    - Encounter "The Offer" event
    - Accept the deal to double your money
  Outcome:
    - Instant double money
    - Banned from all casinos forever
    - Can no longer gamble
    - Life goes on without gambling
  Achievement: "The Deal"
  Narrative: Faustian bargain consequences

----------------------------------------------------------------------
6. SUZY THE SNITCH (Betrayal Ending)
----------------------------------------------------------------------
  Trigger: Complete Suzy storyline on bad relationship path
  Requirements:
    - Met Suzy in early game
    - Made poor choices in her storyline
    - Low relationship with Suzy
  Outcome:
    - Suzy reports you to police for [reasons]
    - Arrested and taken away
    - Game over in handcuffs
  Achievement: "Snitched On"
  Narrative: Consequences of being cruel to innocent girl

----------------------------------------------------------------------
7. THE FACTORY (Dark Ending / Meat Cube Ending)
----------------------------------------------------------------------
  Trigger: Sell 15 companions to Gus at pawn shop
  Requirements:
    - Befriend companions via Animal Whistle
    - Sell them one-by-one to Gus (15 total)
    - Complete all 6 betrayal milestones
  Progression:
    1 sale  → "First Blood" - Gus gives you money, seems normal
    3 sales → "The Product" - Mentions processing plant
    5 sales → "Meat Cubes" - Reveals they become processed meat
    7 sales → "The Menu" - Describes the different flavors
    10 sales→ "Production Run" - Gives you factory business card
    15 sales→ "THE FACTORY" - Full horror revelation
  Outcome:
    - Learn companions are processed into meat cubes
    - "Cube Processing Inc." sends regards
    - Industrial meat processing horror
    - You're complicit in systematic animal processing
  Achievements: 6 betrayal achievements (first_sale → cube_master)
  Narrative: Darkest ending, about commodification of life

19. NEW: FRAUDULENT CASH SYSTEM
================================================================================

**FULLY IMPLEMENTED**: Separate fraudulent money system from loan shark.

----------------------------------------------------------------------
HOW IT WORKS:
----------------------------------------------------------------------
  1. Take loan from Vinnie the loan shark
  2. He gives you FRAUDULENT CASH (not real money)
  3. Vinnie warns: "Gamble with it. Blend it in. No one will know."
  4. Must blend fake cash through gambling to make it real
  5. Dealer accumulates fake cash total
  6. If threshold exceeded, Dealer stops gaining happiness

----------------------------------------------------------------------
BLENDING MECHANICS:
----------------------------------------------------------------------
  - When you bet, you can use real + fraudulent cash combined
  - Game automatically blends fraudulent portion
  - Win or lose, the fraudulent cash becomes real
  - Example: Bet $5,000 ($3,000 real + $2,000 fake)
    → Displays: "(Blending $2,000 fraudulent with $3,000 real money)"
    → After hand, all money is now real
  - Dealer receives and tracks fraudulent cash amounts

----------------------------------------------------------------------
DETECTION THRESHOLDS (Rank-Based):
----------------------------------------------------------------------
  How much fake cash Dealer will accept before suspicion:
  
  Rank 0 (Poor): $50,000 threshold
    → Small transactions don't matter, Dealer isn't careful
    
  Rank 1 (Modest): $30,000 threshold
    → Dealer starts paying more attention
    
  Rank 2 (Well-Off): $15,000 threshold
    → Scrutinizing larger sums
    
  Rank 3 (Rich): $5,000 threshold
    → Very attentive to your money
    
  Rank 4 (Very Rich): $2,000 threshold
    → Expert eye, notices quickly
    
  Rank 5 (Almost Millionaire): $500 threshold
    → VERY suspicious of high roller money
  
  **LOGIC**: When you're poor, passing small fake amounts is easy.
  When you're rich, Dealer scrutinizes large transactions carefully.

----------------------------------------------------------------------
CONSEQUENCES:
----------------------------------------------------------------------
  - Once threshold reached: Dealer stops gaining happiness
  - Warning at casino entrance: "The Dealer looks at you strangely. 
    His jade eye narrows. He's noticed something about your money."
  - Happiness will not increase from wins (stuck at current level)
  - Does NOT affect gambling or bets
  - Can still play, but relationship permanently damaged
  - Contributes to potential Dealer execution ending

----------------------------------------------------------------------
VISUAL FEEDBACK:
----------------------------------------------------------------------
  - Balance display shows: "$5,000 (+ $2,000 fraudulent)"
  - Real money shown in green
  - Fraudulent cash shown in yellow
  - During betting: Shows blend message
  - Clear tracking of fake vs real

----------------------------------------------------------------------
ACHIEVEMENTS:
----------------------------------------------------------------------
  • Money Launderer - Blend $10,000 fraudulent cash
  • Master Launderer - Blend $100,000 total
  • Caught Red-Handed - Dealer stops gaining happiness
  • Fake Money King - Have $500,000 fraudulent at once
