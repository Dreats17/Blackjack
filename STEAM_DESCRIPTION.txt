[SHORT DESCRIPTION]
A darkly compelling roguelike where blackjack is your lifeline, a station wagon is your home, and $1,000,000 is your only way out. Master the cards, survive the streets, and discover which of 8 endings awaits you.

[ABOUT THIS GAME]

You wake up in your car. Again. The casino lights flicker in the distance. Your stomach growls. Your hands shake. You have $47 to your name and a dream that feels more like a curse: win a million dollars, or die trying.

Welcome to the most brutally honest gambling game ever made.

BLACKJACK: THE ROAD TO REDEMPTION is a narrative-driven roguelike that combines hardcore blackjack strategy with deep survival mechanics, branching storylines, and moral choices that matter. Live in your car. Gamble for your life. Meet unforgettable characters. Collect animal companions. Descend into madness. Find redemption. Or lose everything.


🎴 MASTER THE CARDS
• Authentic blackjack with advanced mechanics
• Multiple dealers with unique behaviors and quirks
• Card counting, bet sizing, and risk management
• Casino heat system - win too much and face consequences
• The mysterious Dealer with the jade eye judges your every move


🚗 SURVIVE THE STREETS
• Health, Sanity, Hunger, and Sleep systems
• Your station wagon is shelter, storage, and lifeline
• Scavenge for food, beg for money, or risk everything at the tables
• Dynamic weather and random events threaten your survival
• Balance gambling with basic human needs


🐾 BUILD YOUR FAMILY
• Befriend 32 unique animal companions (dogs, cats, birds, and... an alligator?)
• Each companion provides bonuses and restores your sanity
• Feed them, care for them, name them
• Or betray them for profit and face the darkest ending in gaming


📖 EIGHT PATHS TO DESTINY
• SALVATION: Choose family over fortune and go home
