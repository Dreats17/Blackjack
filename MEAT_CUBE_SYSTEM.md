# The Meat Cube Factory - Dark Companion Betrayal System

## Overview
A progressive dark story system where selling companions to Gus reveals the horrifying truth: they're being processed into meat cubes at an industrial factory.

## Progression System

### Sale Milestones & Revelations

**1 Companion Sold - "First Blood"**
- Gus: "First time's always the hardest. Gets easier after this."
- Achievement: **First Blood** (Common)
- Sanity loss: -15

**3 Companions Sold - "The Product"**
- Gus reveals there's a processing plant out west
- They turn animals into "product"
- Achievement: **The Product** (Uncommon)
- Total sanity loss: -45

**5 Companions Sold - "Meat Cubes"**
- Gus explains: "Meat cubes. That's what they call it."
- Little frozen cubes for pet food... and other uses
- Gus shifts blame: "You BROUGHT them here. I'm just the middleman."
- Achievement: **Meat Cubes** (Rare)
- Total sanity loss: -75

**7 Companions Sold - "The Menu"**
- Gus describes the FLAVORS of different animals
- "Alligator's got a kick to it. Bear's rich, gamey. The little ones - rabbits, cats - those are tender."
- He's describing them like a menu
- Achievement: **The Menu** (Rare)
- Total sanity loss: -105

**10 Companions Sold - "Production Run"**
- Gus gives you a business card: "CUBE PROCESSING INC. - Pet Nutrition Solutions"
- "Ten animals. That's a full production run. They're gonna send you a thank you card."
- A thank you card. For murder.
- Achievement: **Production Run** (Epic)
- Total sanity loss: -150

**15 Companions Sold - "THE FACTORY" (Ending)**
- Final threshold triggered
- Gus reveals the full truth with photos:
  - Massive gray windowless industrial building
  - Production line: stainless steel machinery, conveyor belts
  - Thousands of boxes: "Premium Pet Nutrition Cubes"
- Company processes 200 animals per month
- They offer you a PARTNERSHIP: Supply chain coordinator with percentage of sales
- Business card: "CUBE PROCESSING INC. - WHERE ANIMALS BECOME PRODUCT"
- Achievement: **The Factory** (Legendary)

## The Ending

After selling 15 companions, the player receives:
- Photos of the industrial facility
- Partnership offer from Cube Processing Inc.
- Business card for "Industrial Facility #7"

**That Night at the Casino:**
- The Dealer can smell it: "Betrayal. Blood. Industrial processing."
- "Fifteen souls. Ground into cubes. Packaged. Sold. Consumed."
- "Some people feed the machine."

**The Aftermath:**
- You never call the number, but you never throw away the card
- You hear machinery at night: grinding, processing, cubing
- The scratching at your car door has stopped
- They've been processed
- The factory is always hungry
- And you know where to find more

## Companion Farewell Reactions

Each companion has unique farewell dialogue when taken away:

- **Lucky**: "Lucky's tail wags once. Still trusting. Even now."
- **Whiskers**: "Whiskers hisses and fights. The last sound you hear is her screaming."
- **Grace**: "Grace's eyes hold yours. The fawns cry out. You look away first."
- **Kraken**: "The Kraken's ancient eyes judge you. You will be remembered."
- **Thunder**: "Thunder whinnies once. A sound of betrayal. Of broken trust."
- **Squirrelly**: "Squirrelly drops the acorn they were holding. It rolls toward you. You don't pick it up."
- **Don Coo**: "Don Coo's pigeons scatter in panic. The boss never panics. But he looks back. Once."
- **Others**: "They don't understand. They probably never will."

## Payment Rates

Based on companion rarity:
- **Legendary** (Kraken, Giant Bear, Moon Rabbit, Dolphin): $15,000-$30,000
- **Rare** (Brown Bear, Horse, Deer with fawns): $5,000-$12,000
- **Uncommon** (Alligator, Snake, Racing Crab, Sea Otter, Raccoon Boss, Duck Commander): $2,000-$6,000
- **Common** (Cat, Dog, Squirrel, Seagull, etc.): $500-$3,000

## Sanity System

- Each companion sale: -15 sanity
- By 15 sales: -225 total sanity (player likely broken or near-broken)
- Some companions restore sanity when you back out of the sale (+5)

## Design Philosophy

This system mirrors the collectibles garbling system:
- Progressive revelation of dark truth
- Multiple achievements track your descent
- Each milestone makes it harder to stop
- The ending is inevitable once you start down this path
- It's not about sudden betrayal - it's about slow, willing participation in horror

You're not forced into this. Every sale is a choice. Every companion looks at you as they're taken away. You could stop at any time.

But the factory is always hungry.
