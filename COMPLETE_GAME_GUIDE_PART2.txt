Each ending has a specific trigger sequence:

1. MILLIONAIRE ENDING
   Trigger: Reach $1,000,000
   Event: Visit airport → Choose escape destination

   Trigger: HP reaches 0

   Trigger: Sanity reaches 0
   Event: Reality shatters, asylum scene

4. BROKE ENDING
   Trigger: Day 100+ with <$100 and no prospects
   Event: Forced to leave town
   Cutscene: Hitchhiking away, defeated
   Achievement: "Rock Bottom"

5. DEALER'S DEBT ENDING
   Trigger: Special path with The Dealer
   Event: Make supernatural bargain
   Cutscene: Your soul is claimed
   Achievement: "The House Always Wins"

6. REDEMPTION ENDING
   Trigger: Complete all good karma quests
   Event: Find peace, leave gambling
   Cutscene: Emotional closure
   Achievement: "Redeemed"

7. ESCAPE ENDING  
   Trigger: Flee with moderate money ($10,000-$999,999)
   Event: Leave town at night
   Cutscene: Fresh start elsewhere
   Achievement: "Fresh Start"

8. SECRET ENDING
   Trigger: [CLASSIFIED - Discover yourself]
   Event: Reality breaks, truth revealed
   Cutscene: Mind-bending conclusion
   Achievement: "The Truth"

Each ending has 3-10 minute cutscene/narration.

NG+ unlocked after any ending.
